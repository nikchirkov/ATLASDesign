The Art of Creating Spaces

ATLAS Design is a team of professionals passionate about their craft. We create interiors that become an extension of our clients' personalities, combining functionality with high-end aesthetics.

Every project is a unique story where we serve as guides between your dreams and reality. Our attention to detail, impeccable taste, and years of experience allow us to create spaces that exceed expectations.

Process

How We Work

1. Consultation
Initial meeting to get acquainted, discuss your wishes and understand the task. We study the space and discuss the project budget.

2. Concept
Development of the design concept, including spatial planning, material selection, and color palette.

3. Design
Creation of detailed project documentation, including drawings, 3D visualizations, and material specifications.

4. Execution
Author supervision of project implementation, coordination of contractors and suppliers, quality control of work.
